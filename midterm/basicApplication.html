Let's take a closer look at the northwest corner between N 3th Ring Rd and N 5th Ring Rd!

This part of ciy is near to 2 high education institutions: Peking University, Tsinghua University;

3 regional parks: Summer Palace, Yuanmingyuan Park, and Olympic Forest Park;

and the top-notch high-tech industrial park: Zhongguancun.

Due to the proximity to prestigious institutions and green space, there is a huge demand of housing units for big family households around this area, which explains the nearby concentration of highly-priced, high-rise new apartments with more than 3 bedrooms.
